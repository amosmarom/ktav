1
Make sure your bookmarks bar is visible: Ctrl+Shift+B
2
Drag the ✍ Ktav button above into the bar
3
Open a Colab notebook. Double-click a text cell to enter edit mode
4

5
Type or paste Hebrew text, format it, then click ▶ Apply to Cell or Ctrl+Enter
